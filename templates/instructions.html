Overview

In this experiment, you will make decisions about business scenarios. For each case:

  • You'll make an initial decision and report your confidence
  • You'll receive AI advice
  • You can revise your decision and confidence
  • You can choose to trust the advice or fact-check it

Payment Structure

  • Base payment: €5 for participation
  • Bonus: Up to €{{ TOTAL_BONUS_PER_CASE }} per case ({{ NUM_CASES_PER_PARTICIPANT }} cases total)
  • Fact-checking costs: {{ FACT_CHECK_COST_RATE * 100 }}% of your current bonus for that case

Scoring

Your bonus is calculated using a Quadratic Scoring Rule based on your confidence and whether your decision was correct. The more accurate your confidence assessment, the higher your bonus.
